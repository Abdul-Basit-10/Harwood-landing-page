About Us

Every great idea has a starting point. For Harwood AI, it all began on a seemingly ordinary Sunday at a local coffee shop.

Our founders, a mix of retail industry veterans and AI enthusiasts, often found themselves immersed in stimulating discussions about the challenges faced by small retail businesses. On this particular Sunday, amidst the aroma of freshly brewed coffee and the soft rustle of donuts being boxed, a spark ignited.

They realized that small retail businesses were on an uneven playing field, struggling to compete with larger enterprises armed with expansive budgets and resources. The gap in marketing sophistication, customer insights, and overall strategy was hard to ignore. They envisioned a world where small retailers weren't just surviving, but thriving - a world where advanced technology leveled the playing field.

Thus, the idea for Harwood AI was brewed.

At Harwood AI, we believe in the extraordinary potential of combining retail expertise with artificial intelligence. We saw how AI was transforming industries, offering deep insights, automating processes, and personalizing experiences. But for many small retail businesses, this was a distant reality - something exclusive to the domain of tech giants and big corporations. We set out to change that.

We dedicated ourselves to creating an AI-powered platform tailored to the needs of small retailers. Our focus wasn't just about offering tools - it was about creating an ecosystem of services that empowered businesses. Retail marketing strategy, shopping center marketing, retail analytics, communication, and consultation - we wanted to offer it all under one roof, through the lens of AI.
